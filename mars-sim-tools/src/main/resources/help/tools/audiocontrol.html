Audio Control

The Audio Control provides a tool for managing audio settings in the simulation. There are 2 streams of audio:

  • Music - Continuous background music that plays during the simulation
  • Sound Effects - Short sounds that accompany actions or events in the UI

For each audio stream, it allows users to adjust volume levels, mute or unmute audio.

The Audio Control is a useful tool for quickly finding and adjusting audio settings in the simulation, especially when there are many settings present. It can be used to monitor the status of various audio settings, adjust their levels, and gain insights into the overall audio environment of the simulation.
