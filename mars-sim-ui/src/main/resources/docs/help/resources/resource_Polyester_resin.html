Resource : "Polyester resin" (Liquid)


1. Description :

  • Polyester resins are thermoplastic polymers. It's the most widely used resin systems in the marine industry. By far the majority of dinghies, yachts and workboats built in composites make use of this resin system. Polyesters are produced commercially both as saturated and unsaturated resins. The most common, highest volume produced polyester is Polyethylene terephthalate (PET), a saturated polyester that finds utilization in such applications as fibers for clothing and carpet, food and liquid containers (such as a water/soda bottles), as well as films.
