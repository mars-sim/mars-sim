Tutorial - Step 4 : Mars Navigator Minimap

The Mars Navigator comes with two parts: the Mars globe and the Detail Map. It provides constant updates of your activities on the surface of Mars.

Simply use your mouse to drag around the area inside either the Mars globe or the detailed map to take you where you want to see.


In the detailed map, you may see various names of settlements, landmarks, vehicles and trip marks.

There are a total of 8 data layers that you may toggle on/off on the detailed map.

Note : since the day/night tracking layer may consume a fair bit of processing power to obtain the correct data points for simulating the day/night coverage of Mars, users may consider un-checking it in order to lower the CPU usage.


Currently, there are 10 available minerals that settlers may be able to prospect and mine.

You may toggle on/off the appearance of each mineral on the detailed map.

To see the detail info of a base or a vehicle, hover over it until the crossmark appears. Then click on its overlaying label and its Unit Window will be opened in the Main tab.
