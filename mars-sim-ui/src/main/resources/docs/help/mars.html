Mars

The 4th planet from the Sun, Mars is unsurprisingly the red planet which Mars Simulation Project unfolds on. It has two satellites or 'moons' - Phobos and Deimos.

Mars is both smaller and less dense than Earth, and owes its orange-red hue to ferric oxide (Fe2O3).

The total mass of Mars is around 6.4185·1023 kg, or about one tenth of Earth's.

The atmosphere is composed mostly of carbon dioxide (CO2), accounting for 95.32% of the total atmosphere. While unbreathable by humans, this is utilized in some of the settlements' processes.
