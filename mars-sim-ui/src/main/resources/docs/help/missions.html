Missions

Missions are outdoor expeditions undertaken by settlers.

Normally, the settlement prioritizes what missions to conduct at a given time based on its needs.

Also, all ongoing missions are tabulated at the Mission tab in the Monitor Tool.

While Missions are generated automatically by mars-sim, user may also create any particular missions manually via the Create Mission Wizard.

The 12 types of Missions are as follows :

  • Areology Study Field Mission
  • Biology Study Field Mission
  • Building Construction Mission
  • Building Salvage Mission
  • Collect Ice
  • Collect Regolith
  • Emergency Supply Mission
  • Exploration
  • Mining
  • Rescue Salvage Vehicle
  • Trade
  • Travel To Settlement
